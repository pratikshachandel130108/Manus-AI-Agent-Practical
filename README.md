Planning a trip to Canada can be overwhelming due to its vast geography, multiple cities, and countless attractions.

-Local experiences
-Transportation guidance
-Budget estimation
-Time optimization

Whether you're a solo traveler, couple, or group — this itinerary helps you make the most of your Canada trip.

## Travel Tips
-Book flights early for cheaper prices
-Use public transport (metro/bus) in cities
-Carry valid travel insurance
-Always check weather before packing
-Keep some cash + international card
-Respect local rules and nature zones
